      5th workshop on Medical Physics and Biomedical Engineering

      The topics of the workshop include

      • Hybrid Imaging and Personalized Medicine
      • Radiation Protection in Medical Physics
      • Innovation in Medical Physics Techniques.

      The Corfu Workshop on will continue the series of workshops previously held in different countries in Eastern Europe: Sarajevo-2014 in Bosnia, Shkodra-2014 in Albania, Ohrid- 2015 in Macedonia, and Elbasan-2016 in Albania. The workshop signals the 5th Satellite event of GeNeDis 2018. More information about the workshop and its program will be announced closer to the event.
